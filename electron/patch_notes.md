### Version 1.0.1

---

### News & Improvements
- **Interactive Log Viewer**: Console logs are now in full color! Errors are highlighted in red, warnings in yellow, and info messages are dimmed. Easily copy or clear logs with the new buttons directly in the log panel.
- **Search Logs**: You can now search and filter logs live to track down errors even faster.
- **RAM Recommendation System**: Use the new **Recommended** option to auto-detect and allocate the optimal RAM for your system.
- **Fluent Glow Effects**: Instance cards on the dashboard now react with a modern, radial cursor-following glow effect.
- **Mod Duplicate Scanner**: The launcher scans your mods folder before launching, warns you about duplicate jar versions, and can automatically remove older ones to prevent crashes.
- **Launch Status Toast**: Starting the game updates a smooth, non-blocking toast notification that updates in real-time.
- **Quilt Modloader Support**: Full out-of-the-box support for Quilt modpacks and instances.
